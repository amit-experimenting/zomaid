D · Trust Utility

Bank/utility-app reliability. Square corners, civic blue, tabular numerals, plain sans. Reads as dependable and serious — great for bills/money flows, dry for cooking.

My recommendation: A (Warm Home). Two reasons. First, the maid persona — warm/earthy reads as welcoming and approachable, which matters for users with lower app-fluency far more than it does for tech-savvy owners. Second, the domain — this app is about food, family, daily rituals. A "calm modern" or "trust utility" treatment of "Dal Tadka" feels emotionally off. Optionally borrow one trait from D (tabular numerals) for the bills section.
